# Swift Motors – Vehicle Manufacturing & Quality Analytics

## Overview

This project was completed as part of the IBM Cognos Analytics Virtual Internship. The objective was to analyze manufacturing operations and quality metrics for Swift Motors using IBM Cognos Analytics. The project focused on production monitoring, defect analysis, downtime tracking, supplier performance evaluation, and executive-level business reporting.

## Business Scenario

Swift Motors is a leading automobile manufacturing company with production plants located across multiple locations. The organization aims to improve production efficiency, reduce defect rates, minimize downtime, and optimize supplier performance through data-driven decision-making.

## Tools & Technologies

* IBM Cognos Analytics
* Business Intelligence (BI)
* Data Visualization
* Dashboard Development
* Reporting & Analytics

## Key Tasks Performed

### Production Quality Reporting

* Created reports to analyze total units produced, defective units, and defect rates.
* Applied conditional formatting to identify quality issues across manufacturing plants.

### Downtime Analysis

* Developed reports to monitor assembly line downtime.
* Identified production hours lost due to operational interruptions.

### Defect Analysis

* Visualized defect type distribution across plants.
* Analyzed quality trends and recurring defect patterns.

### Exploratory Analytics

* Investigated relationships between shift types, defect rates, assembly lines, supervisors, and suppliers.
* Identified operational factors influencing manufacturing quality.

### Manufacturing Operations Dashboard

* Designed interactive KPI dashboards displaying:

  * Total Units Produced
  * Defect Rate
  * Downtime Hours
  * Scrap Units
* Built visualizations for production trends, supplier performance, and quality metrics.

### Executive Storytelling

* Developed a business presentation using Cognos Stories to communicate:

  * Production performance
  * Quality analysis
  * Downtime impact
  * Supplier evaluation
  * Improvement recommendations
